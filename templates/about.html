Intentional Spending Tracker

The Intentional Spending Tracker is a personal finance tool designed to help you align your spending with your values. By following the popular 50/30/20 Rule, you can ensure your needs are met, your future is secure, and you still have room to enjoy life today.

The 50/30/20 Framework

Divide your after-tax income into three buckets:

  • Fixed Costs (50%): Needs. Rent/Mortgage, utilities, groceries, transportation, and insurance. These are bills you must pay.
  • Fun Spending (30%): Wants. Dining out, shopping, hobbies, Netflix, and travel. This is the money you enjoy!
  • Future You (20%): Savings & Debt. Emergency fund, retirement accounts (401k/IRA), and paying down high-interest debt.

How to Use This Tracker

  1. Click the button below to visit the Dashboard.
  2. Add your Income (use your net/take-home pay).
  3. Log your Expenses as they happen.
  4. Assign each expense to Fixed, Fun, or Future.
  5. Watch the charts! If a bar turns red, you've exceeded your percentage for that bucket. Adjust your spending to get back on track.
